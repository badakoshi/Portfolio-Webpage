About Alhassan Muhammad Bello

I am Alhassan Muhammad Bello — a dedicated Computer Educator, certified Examiner, and Online Coding Tutor bridging classrooms in Nigeria with learners across the United Kingdom.

At Garima Standard Academy, I teach Digital Technology, equipping students with practical computing skills and digital literacy. My role as an Examiner gives me unique insight into what assessors value.

Beyond the classroom, I run an Online Coding Tutorship for UK students, delivering live interactive sessions in programming fundamentals and project-based coding.

What Defines My Approach

  • Dual Context: Nigerian education roots + UK online delivery experience.
  • Practical Focus: Every lesson connects to real-world digital skills.
  • Student-Centered: The learner always comes first, in-person or online.
